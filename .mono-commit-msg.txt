Partner Hub: Partners list for super admin - pick a partner to work in its hub with full access (ASCII-only)

NO MIGRATION REQUIRED. Frontend only, seeded partner data.

New Partners page (/partner/partners, super-admin only) lists every partner on the platform (TalentForge, SkillBridge) with orgs, seats, funders, staff, delegated admins and funding value. Selecting a partner sets it active (setActivePartner) and opens /partner - and because getPartnerHub now resolves the active partner when the viewer has no partnerId of their own (i.e. a super admin), the WHOLE Partner Hub (Overview, Organisations, Financial Hub, Funders, Documents, Accounts, Communications, Audit, and every org/class beneath) resolves to that partner, giving full access to its dependencies. A real partner_admin always sees only their own partner (override ignored). Partners added to the super-admin nav (default sidebar + in-hub sidebar).
