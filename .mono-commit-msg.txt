Course Development Suite: Review and Publish tab (review real content, videos, activity previews, publish) (ASCII-only)

The suite ended at authoring drafts with no way to review the finished course or finalize it. New Review and Publish tab: pick a real course, then see a readiness checklist (description, modules, published module, activities, published activities, an assessment), the module list (open each to review its content + video), interactive activities PREVIEWED INLINE (ActivityPlayer), and the attached case studies + assignments. A Publish course button (PATCH /courses status) with an Unpublish toggle finalizes it. Reads existing endpoints (/courses, /courses/:id, /activities?courseId, /courses/:id/cases, /courses/:id/assignments).
