Real org courses table (replaces synthetic placeholder data)

The org Courses tab was rendering fabricated courses from orgCourses() in partnerHubData (fake titles, fake enrolled counts, ids like <orgId>_c0), so its ids never matched the real class courseIds and 'In classes' always showed a dash. New GET /organisations/:orgId/courses returns the org's REAL courses - the union of courses attached to its classes (org_class_courses) and courses its members are enrolled in - with real enrolled counts and completion-based avgProgress, real titles and status. PartnerOrgHub now queries that endpoint instead of orgCourses(); the Courses table, the Course-averages table, and the 'In classes' chips are all real and share one id space, so chips populate. Files: routes/classes.ts, pages/partner/PartnerOrgHub.tsx.
