Module polish: render reading markdown, linear nav, drop mastery block, open-able case studies (ASCII-only)

Five follow-up fixes to the built-out modules. (1) Readings now render Markdown properly (headings, bold, lists) via a new dependency-free MarkdownView instead of showing literal # and ** - and the reader is restyled as a clean, centred article. (2) Same change makes reading pages look professional. (3) The bottom Next button now walks strictly FORWARD, section by section, in order (Readings -> Complete -> Case studies -> ...), never jumping back. (4) Removed the Demonstrate-mastery block entirely (rail item, Complete-tab card, Structure row, and the mastery states in the nav) - completion just follows working through the sections. (5) Case studies now open: they were 404ing because the case was owned by the content org while learners sit in a different org - enzaEnrich now creates/repairs them as LIBRARY cases (organisationId null, isLibrary true, published), which the module's course-enrolment check gates; and the Case Studies tab is redesigned as a full card with a Start case study button (with error surfacing).
