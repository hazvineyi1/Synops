Configurable gradebook P1: grade types, sync-all-activities, auto-score from completion

Fixes the partner-review finding where a completed course showed GRADED assignments with no grade. (1) New per-item grade_type on gradebook_items (points | pass_fail | completion), healed at boot in dbHardening; engine surfaces it per column (completion columns always render as %). (2) New POST /courses/:courseId/gradebook/sync registers EVERY course deliverable (activities, cases, workshops, per-module completion) as a gradebook column, idempotent - generalises the enzaEnrich per-module loop so the gradebook stays in sync with all activities. Assignments already appear by default. (3) Auto-score from completion %: computeLearner now fills any ungraded SUMMATIVE item from the learner's course-completion fraction (flagged auto), so no graded row shows a blank dash. FE: MyGrades renders Pass/Fail, %, X/Y per grade_type with an 'auto' chip; CourseGradebook gets a 'Sync activities' button. Phase 2 (admin/org config UI) to follow. Files: schema/gradebook.ts, dbHardening.ts, gradebookEngine.ts, routes/gradebook.ts, MyGrades.tsx, gradebookApi.ts, CourseGradebook.tsx.
