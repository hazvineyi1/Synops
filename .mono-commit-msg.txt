Super-admin Document Library: standard legal pack stored on platform + sendable to partners (ASCII-only)

Synops's standard document pack (MSA, Partner/Reseller, MOU, DPA, Order Form/pricing, SLA, AUP, NDA, Learner Terms, plus the index) is now stored on the platform and distributable. New documentTemplates router (mounted) serving a baked documentTemplates.json (dash-free HTML): GET /platform/document-templates (list, super admin), GET /document-templates/:key (content, any authed user), POST /platform/document-templates/:key/send (files the doc into each selected partner's Documents and Filing), GET .../recipients. New super-admin Document Library page (/admin/document-templates, in the Platform nav): view any document and Send-to-partners (multi-select, skips partners who already have it). Partner Documents and Filing gains a View button for template-backed rows. Added template_key column to partner_documents. Separately: the letterhead Word/PDF pack in the Legal Templates folder was regenerated with ALL em/en dashes removed.
