Super-admin Delete partner: cascade-remove a partner and all its data (ASCII-only)

Adds DELETE /partners/:id (super admin) which hard-deletes a partner and everything scoped to it: its organisations, all users under it, their enrolments/progress/grades/coaching/attendance/sessions, its classes, org-scoped activities/cases, partner-OWNED courses and content, and every partner-scoped record (brand theme, course assignments, delegated admins, funders, billing, documents, announcements, filings). Platform-owned shared courses are kept (only the assignment is removed). No FK constraints, so each DELETE is independent + wrapped in try/catch. Partner Management gains a Delete button per row with a confirm. Lets the founder remove the demo partners (TalentForge, SkillBridge, Test 123) and keep only Enza Global Media.

VALIDATION: esbuild clean on partners.ts, AdminPartners.tsx.
