Fix notification 404: assignment notifications linked to a non-existent route (ASCII-only)

A graded-assignment notification linked to /assignments/:id, but the only route was /courses/:courseId/assignments/:id - so clicking it 404'd. Fixed the notification link in applyGrade to include the course id, and added a fallback route /assignments/:assignmentId that resolves the course from the assignment itself (so older notifications with the bare link also work). AssignmentDetail now derives courseId from the route OR the loaded assignment.
