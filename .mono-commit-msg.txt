AI white-label brand kit: logo/card/website -> Claude vision derives colours + personalization (ASCII-only)

Backend: POST /brand/ai-generate (super_admin/partner_admin) sends the uploaded logo (+ optional business card image, website, business name) to Claude vision and returns a brand kit JSON: displayName, primaryColor/secondaryColor/accentColor (validated hex), fontFamily, credentialTitle, tagline. Does not save - the client previews then applies via the existing PUT /brand/theme. 503 if AI env not configured.

Frontend (Branding page / PartnerTheme): new AI brand kit card - upload logo (stored as a data URL in logoUrl, no external storage needed) + optional business card + website + name -> Generate brand with AI -> fills the colour/name/font/credential fields and live preview; then Save applies it. Manual colour editing still works if AI is off.

Needs AI env (AI_INTEGRATIONS_ANTHROPIC_BASE_URL + _API_KEY) for generation; branding otherwise works manually.

VALIDATION: esbuild clean on brand.ts and PartnerTheme.tsx.
