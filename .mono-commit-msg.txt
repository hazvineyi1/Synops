Error-pattern sweep: stop pages hanging on a skeleton when a query 403s/500s (ASCII-only)

Audited every learner/staff page for the 'loading UI gated on the ABSENCE of data' anti-pattern (isLoading || !data, or !data && Skeleton). On a 403/500 TanStack sets isLoading=false but leaves data undefined, so these spin the skeleton forever. Fixed each to capture isError (retry:false) and render a clear error/empty state instead of an endless skeleton.

Learner-facing: LearnSession (tutoring), CaseBegin, CaseSession, Support ticket detail. Staff/partner: CourseDetail (Announcements/Pages/People tabs), CoachingMatching MatchingBoard, CoachSettings, CaseBuilder, PlatformConsole overview, CourseDevelopmentSuite modules. Each now shows a 'could not load' message with a way back rather than hanging.

VALIDATION: esbuild clean on all 10 changed pages.
