Testing: enrolment rollup unit tests (extract pure org-course aggregation)

Extracts the org-courses enrolment rollup out of the GET /organisations/:orgId/courses route into a pure, DB-free lib (orgCourseAgg.ts): orgCourseIds (union + dedup of class-attached OR member-enrolled courses) and aggregateOrgCourses (enrolled counts, completion-based progress from status OR completedAt, published->active/else->draft, sorted). The route now calls these (behaviour-preserving) and 8 new unit tests cover the edge cases (overlap dedup, zero-enrolment class course, progress rounding, per-course attribution, dropping deleted courses). Runs in CI via the existing build-praxis vitest gate. Files: lib/orgCourseAgg.ts, lib/orgCourseAgg.test.ts, routes/classes.ts.
