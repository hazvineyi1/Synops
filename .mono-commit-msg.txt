Super admin: Platform Overview - all partners at a glance with orgs/funders/financials + overall platform financials (ASCII-only)

NO MIGRATION REQUIRED. Frontend only, seeded partner data.

The super admin Overview now points to a new Platform Overview (/platform-overview). It aggregates across every partner (new platformOverview() helper): platform KPIs (partners, organisations, seats, funders), an overall Platform Financials card (monthly recurring incl VAT, outstanding, funding value, VAT collected, funder received - summed across all partners), and a per-partner breakdown. Each partner is an expandable row showing its organisations (click to open that org), funders (value + status) and a financial-hub snapshot, with Open hub / Open Financial Hub buttons that set the active partner and drop into their hub. Clicking a partner or org enters that partner via the active-partner switch.
