Add + publish activities inside a module (ModuleViewer Complete tab) (ASCII-only)

Inside a module's Complete section, instructors now get an Activities in this module panel: Add existing (attach a library/other activity to this course+module via PATCH), New (author one), and per activity a Publish activity button (PATCH published:true) plus Unpublish and Remove-from-module. Draft activities are marked and only surface to learners once published. This makes the module the place deliverables are added and completed, with a clear publish step.
