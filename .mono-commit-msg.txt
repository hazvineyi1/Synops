Resync Learner Progress: re-point demo learners at current content, fix off-track + notifications (ASCII-only)

Rebuilding courses (Build Full Courses) gives beats new IDs, which orphaned the seeded learners' progress - so completion read artificially low and an advanced learner could be wrongly flagged as falling behind. Partner Management gains a Resync Learner Progress button.
